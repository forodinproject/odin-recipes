Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 quarters half-and-half cream
  • 0.5 pint heavy cream
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  1. Combine half-and-half, cream, sugar, vanilla and salt in freezer container of ice cream maker.
  2. Freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
